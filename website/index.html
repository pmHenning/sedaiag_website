Business idea

Develop AI-supported software that enables better investment decisions through a holistic view of real estate and the market

In all investment decisions in the real estate industry, whether in the context of transactions, management or restructuring, transparency regarding the relevant data is a decisive key to success. This applies to both the building data itself, but also market information, regulations and strategic foresight

That’s why software developers, real estate experts, financial experts and technical consultants work hand in hand at SedAI.

SedAI has created a technology that enables investors to map the sustainability of the entire value chain not only of individual properties, but also of entire portfolios or even cities and municipalities with minimal data requirements. This allows both individual strategic decisions and the effects of changes in policy or regulation to be modeled and included in the decision-making process. This allows investors and political decision-makers to make the consequences of strategies and regulations visible and to minimize risks.

PROBLEM

One of the world’s most important industries sees itself at risk in its economic model.

ESG Regulatory Requirements

Get tougher every year and are applicable to more and more companies. Not compliant -> Bad reputation (We need text here) The legal regulation by ESG not only affects the renovation and management of existing buildings from the point of view of energy efficiency, but also their evaluation and financing.

Devaluation

Trend towards more sustainability and social responsibility, supported by the ESG regulation, leads to one of the most significant transformations and disruption of the real estate industry with a significant impact on its economic value (devaluations from currently 15% to total default) and its social impact (market value, rentableness).

High Interest Rates

Due to ESG regulatory requirements, banks are increasingly offering more favorable credit conditions for real estate financing that meet these ESG criteria. Non-ESG-compliant real estate will no longer be eligible for lending in the future (by banks and institutional funds/investors, etc.).

Strategic Investment Uncertainty

The real estate industry lacks the data and parameters to make the right strategic investment and restructuring decisions in the face of this massive threat to their business model. A multi-billion industry that has only known the upward trend for decades is an analog industry. There was no need for digitization.

SOLUTION

AI-based software to make strategically correct decisions regarding ESG criteria and regulation with minimal data effort.

A software developed by English AI specialists allows individual strategic investment factors as well as the impact of changes in policy or regulation to be included in the decision-making process. This is done by means of AI-driven proprietary algorithms and statistical models that are able to create automated and targeted ESG DDs, strategies, analyses and reports on the basis of a weak data situation. In this way, real estate investors and policy makers can make the consequences of ESG criteria and regulations visible on the basis of minimal data and thus significantly minimize their risks."
